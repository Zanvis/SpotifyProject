About Our Project

Music App is a school project created by a team of music enthusiasts and student developers. Our goal is to provide a seamless and enjoyable music listening experience while giving users complete control over their music library.

This project showcases our team's skills in web development, user interface design, and backend implementation. We've worked together to create a platform that demonstrates our understanding of modern web technologies and user-centered design principles.
